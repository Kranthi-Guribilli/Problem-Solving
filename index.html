About:

Welcome to a place where you can learn basic problem solving at your own pace. The primary aim is to build a problem-solving mindset. Throughout the process just be without any specific goals or intentions; simply do it for the love of programming. Anything else you gain from it is a bonus. No pressure, just fun!

Why C Language?

Let's start with C, a language that teaches you the basics without relying too much on fancy tools. No library support! We are writing our own functions and everything from scratch. It's like building with Lego blocks. we call it "The Hard Way". We believe this approach not only makes learning more interesting and challenging but also helps you become a better problem solver. So, dive in and enjoy the journey!
